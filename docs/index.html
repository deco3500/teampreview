Features

Adjusted Scores

Before The Trailers takes into account reviewer's history and creates an adjusted score based on similar movies and genres they have reviewed. This will allow Users to quickly check a review to find out how the reviewer feels this movie stacks up against others like it.

Follow Your Friends

Follow you friends to keep up with movies they see and reviews they write on your personally curated feed.

Get Movie Recommendations

Before the Trailers gets to known you from a movie taste test. Simply let it know which movies you like and it will create your personalized suggestions.

If you tastes ever change you can simply take the taste test again.

Become a Reviewer

Become a reviewer and write your own reviews. Write accurate reviews and build your reviewer following.
